  1. trait DefaultReproduction extends ReproductionStrategy

    Default EMAS reproduction strategy.

    Default EMAS reproduction strategy. Two children agents are created out of two parent ones. A binary genetic operator is used to create the children solutions. The parents also give some of their energy to the children.

    Parameters:

    • emas.reproductionTransfer - the amount of energy to transfer from parent to child.
